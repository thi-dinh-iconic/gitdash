## What was built

### `src/app/api/github/team-stats/route.ts`
- Fetches up to 100 completed runs across all workflows via `listWorkflowRunsForRepo`
- Aggregates per contributor: runs, success, failure, success rate, avg duration, avg queue wait
- Computes activity heatmap: 24-bucket hour-of-day array + 7-bucket day-of-week array
- Identifies busiest hour (UTC)
- Identifies top contributor (most runs) and most reliable contributor (highest success rate, ≥5 runs)
- Returns `TeamStatsResponse` with `contributors[]`, `total_runs`, `period_days`, `top_contributor`, `most_reliable`
- Cache-Control: `private, s-maxage=120, stale-while-revalidate=120`
